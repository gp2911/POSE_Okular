2 /*
3  * The layout of a font information block.
4  * There is one of these for every loaded font or magnification thereof.
5  * Duplicates are eliminated: this is necessary because of possible recursion
6  * in virtual fonts.
7  *
8  * Also note the strange units. The design size is in 1/2^20 point
9  * units (also called micro-points), and the individual character widths
10  * are in the TFM file in 1/2^20 ems units, i.e., relative to the design size.
11  *
12  * We then change the sizes to SPELL units (unshrunk pixel / 2^16).
13  */
